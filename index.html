The major 4 Steps in the Events of the Universe

Universe sprang into existence from a 'Singularity'

The term 'Singularity' physicists use to describe regions of space that defy the laws of physics is thought to be the cause of the Big Bang.

New Born Universe

The infant Universe was initially so hot and dense that even elementary particles such as protons and neutrons could not exist. Instead, different types of matter (called matter and anti-matter) collided together, creating pure energy.

Expanding Universe

The universe appears to be expanding at an increasing rate, so that the velocity at which a distant galaxy is receding from the observer is continuously increasing with time.

The Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation (CMB)

When a microwave telescope is pointed around the sky, it detects a faint microwave glow.The CMB is the afterglow of the Big Bang, the event that set our universe in motion.
